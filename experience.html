The Experience

  • Category: Game development
  • Language: Blueprint (C++)
  • Project date: March 2022 - September 2022
  • Video: youtube

Introduction

The experience is an asymmetrical multiplayer-game, really inspired by Dead By Daylight and Evolve This is my first experience in autonomy on unreal engine 5. I tried to use everything that I self-taught : gameplay, interaction, level-design, sound, animation...

Scenario

3 students are trapped in an abandoned university building, against one monster. 9 player characters divide into 3 classes : runner, support, detector) with special abilities. For example : invisibility, increased speed , door locking , target detection.

Students : survive and leave the building Monster : kill everyone
